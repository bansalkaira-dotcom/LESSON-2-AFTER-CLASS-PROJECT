MY SCHOOL- DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL,CHANDIGARH



Delhi Public School, Chandigarh is one of the leading educational institutions in the city, affiliated with the prestigious Delhi Public School Society. The school is committed to providing holistic education, focusing not only on academics but also on the overall development of students. It aims to create an environment where children can explore their talents, grow in confidence, and develop essential life skills.

The school follows the CBSE curriculum, which ensures a strong foundation in subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages. Experienced and dedicated teachers use innovative teaching methods to encourage creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities in students.

Key Features of DPS

Academic Excellence:

Regular assessments and interactive learning methods help students achieve outstanding academic results.

Modern Infrastructure:

The campus has spacious classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, auditoriums, and extensive playgrounds.

Co-Curricular and Extracurricular Activities:

Students participate in music, dance, drama, art, debates, and a variety of sports such as cricket, football, and basketball.
